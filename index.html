Research & Methodology

This plan is designed to safely guide you to a sub-20 minute 5K target using the scientifically validated Polarized Training Model (80/20 Rule).

By keeping 80% of your runs at a highly conversational pace, you build massive aerobic capacity and strengthen connective tissues while keeping injury risk extremely low. The remaining 20% focuses on progressive high-intensity efforts to push your lactate threshold and improve running economy.
